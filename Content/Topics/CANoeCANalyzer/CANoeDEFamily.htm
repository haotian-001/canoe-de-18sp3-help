CANoe DE Family

CANoe DE

CANoe DE/CANoe4SW DE is the comprehensive software tool for the development of entire ECU networks as well as individual ECUs and is primarily designed for the automotive sector and related industries.

The comprehensive functions for simulation, testing, analysis and diagnostics support network designers, development and test engineers at OEMs and suppliers.

In contrast toCANoe DE, CANoe4SW DE does not require a real hardware connection, as it focuses on pure software systems that are developed on virtual execution environments on the computer, in virtual machines or in the cloud.

CANoe4SW DE

CANoe:lite DE Family

CANoe:lite DE

CANoe:lite DE/CANoe4SW:lite DE is the comprehensive software tool for developing complete networked systems or individual system components and is primarily designed for markets such as medical technology, rail technology and IoT.

The comprehensive functions for simulation, testing and analysis support network designers, development and test engineers.

In contrast to CANoe:lite DE,CANoe4SW:lite DE does not require a real hardware connection, as it focuses on pure software systems that are developed on virtual execution environments on the computer, in virtual machines or in the cloud.
CANoe4SW:lite DE
