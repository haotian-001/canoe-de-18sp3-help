Use Cases

What is the CFS for?

The CFS extends the connection functions of your CANoe DE product. With the CFS you currently have the possibility to connect Connected Devices to your CANoe DE product via the MQTT protocol, for example to simulate and test Internet-of-Things (IoT) use cases. This connection can be made either via a local broker (see use cases 1, 2) or via the internal CANoe DE product Broker (see use case 1).

Use Case 1 – “Local” Broker Device

In this use case, a device connects to a local broker running on a laptop or connected device, for example. Either an external MQTT broker can be used or the internal broker provided by your CANoe DE product.

Use Case 2 – CAN -> MQTT Gateway (only available for CANoe DE and CANoe:lite DE)

Here your CANoe DE product acts as a gateway to send data from various sensors, implemented e.g. by a LIN/CAN ECUs, to an external broker in the network or cloud. This data can then be processed by a connected device.
