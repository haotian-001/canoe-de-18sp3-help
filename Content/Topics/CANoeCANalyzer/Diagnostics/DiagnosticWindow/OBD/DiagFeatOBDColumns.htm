Columns (OBD - Diagnostic Feature)

Ribbon|Diagnostics & XCP Tab|Diagnostic Window|OBD

Valid for:  CANoe DE

The columns of the Diagnostic Feature Fault Memory can be displayed individually. Display the Column Chooser by a right click on the column header and select the desired columns. You can also change the position of the columns by drag and drop.

The columns have the following meaning:
