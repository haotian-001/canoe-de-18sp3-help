VT2828 – Signal Switch Matrix Module

The VT2828 Signal Switch Matrix Module allows arbitrary switching of 16 columns (channels) with 8 rows (16×8 switch matrix).

It is possible to switch high frequency differential signals like CAN, CAN FD, CAN XL, FlexRay, LIN, or 10Base-T1S.
