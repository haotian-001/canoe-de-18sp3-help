Feature Availability

 

Valid for:  CANoe DE  •  CANoe:lite DE  •  CANoe4SW DE  •  CANoe4SW:lite DE

A detailed overview of all available functions of your product can be found in the Feature Matrix.

CANoe DE

  • Support of automotive networks
  • Support of real and simulated networks
  • Support of hardware connection

CANoe SE

  • Completely automated execution of configurations in a server environment based on Windows
  • Support of real and simulated networks
  • Support of hardware connection

CANoe4SW DE – currently not available

CANoe4SW SE

  • Completely automated execution of configurations in a server environment based on Windows or Linux.
  • Support of simulated networks
  • No support of hardware connection

CANoe:lite DE

  • No support of automotive networks
  • No support of network symbols such as signals, frames
  • Hardware connection to VIO System, VT System, IOT Enabler, Port Link, VN devices are only partially supported

CANoe:lite SE – currently not available

CANoe4SW:lite DE

  • No support of automotive networks
  • No support of network symbols such as signals, frames
  • No support of hardware connection

CANoe4SW:lite SE – currently not available
